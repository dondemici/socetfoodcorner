Welcome to Socet Food Corner!

We are dedicated to serving up delicious, nutritious, and affordable meals for students of all ages. Whether you're a grade schooler craving a quick bite, a high school student looking for a tasty lunch, or a university scholar needing a refreshing break, our menu has something special just for you. Our wide variety of rice meals are packed with flavor and balanced nutrition, perfect for fueling your day. From classic favorites to exciting new creations, each dish is prepared with care and the finest ingredients to ensure you enjoy every bite.

Our commitment to quality goes beyond our food. We understand that students lead busy lives, so we've designed our space to be a cozy and convenient spot where you can unwind between classes or catch up with friends. Our friendly staff is always ready to welcome you with a smile and ensure you have a pleasant dining experience. Whether you're dining in or taking out, you can count on Socet Food Corner to provide a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like a second home.

Don't forget to pair your meal with one of our irresistible milkshakes! Our milkshakes come in a variety of flavors, crafted to satisfy your sweet tooth and provide a refreshing complement to our savory rice dishes. At Socet Food Corner, we believe in creating a welcoming environment where you can relax, enjoy great food, and make lasting memories with friends. Join us today and discover why we're the favorite spot for students in the community. We can't wait to serve you!
